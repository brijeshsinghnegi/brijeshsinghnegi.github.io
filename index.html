What is Bench Sales?

Bench Sales is a specialized area of IT staffing where a recruiter markets available IT consultants who are between projects to clients and prime vendors. It requires deep understanding of the US IT staffing ecosystem, C2C contracts, and technology domains.

🎯

My Role as a Bench Sales Recruiter

I proactively reach out to vendors and direct clients to market bench consultants. I handle end-to-end placement from sourcing requirements, submitting profiles, scheduling interviews, negotiating rates, and ensuring smooth onboarding onto projects.

🤝

Vendor and Client Relationships

Over 5 years, I have built strong relationships with prime vendors and direct clients across the US. I understand C2C, W2, and 1099 contracts, ensuring compliance while maximizing placement success rates across multiple technology stacks.

⚡

Why I Deliver Results

With a 90% joining rate across 20+ placements, my approach combines aggressive marketing, thorough screening, and precise client matching. I specialize in niche technologies like Camunda BPM, Maximo, and Power BI.
